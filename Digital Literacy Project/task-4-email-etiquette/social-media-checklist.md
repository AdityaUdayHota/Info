# Do's in social media
- do verify your followers in order to avoid bots.
- do use strong passwords for security in personal privacy.
- do engage in authentic conversations without losing your posture.
- do priortize high-quality content for a good time.
# Dont's in social media
- don't engage in over-emotional conversations which will be futile in the end.
- don't click suspicious links as they can be hacked.
- don't use public wi-fi as they are easily vulnerable to scams.
- don't use hashtags in repeat as they can be considered to be bots.
- don't share your personal information in the internet.
