# How To Stay Safe Online
- Don't give up your personal information on online portal unless absolutely required. To see if required, look for the star after a question.
- Create unusual or creative passwords with more than 12 characters, each one different for different accounts for personal data security.
- Don't click suspicious links or buttons on the screen, as that generally lead to financial fraud.
- Don't scan QR in the hope of receiving money because they are only used for sending money.
- Update your software systems in constant basis to protect your data from constant cyber-attacks.
- Use truted VPN when using public wi-fi for important tasks in private accounts.
- Check the privacy settings in your social media accounts whenever required.
- To report a cybercrime, use national cyber-crime portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and helpline 1930.
